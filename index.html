Family brief history

The Jam Gbinde family hails from Mbaasuku, Mbatsen, Gaav, Konshisha Local Government Area of Benue State, Nigeria, family is headed by HRH. Thomas Tyover Jam Gbinde who is the traditonal ruler from Konshisha Local Government, he is a second class chief from Tiv land

Tyover Jam Gbinde was born by mother, Mama Ashieahen alongside two (2) junior brother,
They are:

  • HRH. Thomas Tyover Jam Gbinde
  • Mr. Ijirtamen Jam Gbinde
  • Mr. Joseph Jam Gbinde

They all grow up to reproduce families as follows:
